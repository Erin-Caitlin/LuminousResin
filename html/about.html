About

Welcome to Luminous Resin, a testament to my creative vision as its sole proprietor, Erin Caitlin Williams. Established in 2020, Luminous Resin emerged from my passion for crafting and a yearning to channel my artistic talents into a thriving business. Inspired by resin artistry's allure, I set out to create a space where creativity knows no bounds, transforming the ordinary into the extraordinary.

What began as a personal exploration in my backyard has blossomed into a dedicated endeavor, fueled by a profound appreciation for the transformative power of resin art; with a deep-seated love, I embarked on my entrepreneurial journey handcrafting resin keychains, each with a unique charm and radiance, and it's this inherent ability to imbue objects with a luminous quality that defines the essence of Luminous Resin.
